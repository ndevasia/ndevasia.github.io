Hey there, I'm Nisha! I love video games and talking robots, and have sought them out professionally in industry and research settings. I'm currently in the process of finishing up my B.S. in Comparative Media Studies and Computer Science at MIT, and working on deploying a variety of playful classroom experiences through my work at the MIT Media Lab.



Reach out at ndevasia@mit.edu

Skills

Programming

Interned at gaming giants PlayStation and Twitch, with full time conversion to backend engineer at the latter. 3+ years of rigorous coding experience in Python, Java, C++, and HTML/CSS.

Designing

3+ years of designing for various playful experiences; created my own games in Unity, prototyped UI tools for game developers, and written interactive curricula for K-12 students.

Writing

Fledling researcher with many publications in the works. More informally, maintained blogs where I blast all my thoughts to the Internet since 2011, both personally and professionally.
